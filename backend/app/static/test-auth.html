Troubleshooting Guide

Issue: "Registration works but login fails" Solution: 1. Check email for verification link 2. Click the verification link 3. Try login again --- Issue: "Page doesn't load after login" Solution: 1. Check browser console for errors (F12) 2. Check that localStorage has 'user_token' and 'user_data' 3. Verify token is not empty --- Issue: "Token not being saved" Solution: 1. Check that auth.html saves to 'user_token' (not 'token') 2. Check browser localStorage is enabled 3. Clear browser cache and try again
